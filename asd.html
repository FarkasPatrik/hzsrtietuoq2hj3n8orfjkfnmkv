What is Fortnite?

Fortnite is a popular battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing.

How to Play

Players drop from a battle bus onto an island to loot weapons and items. The safe zone gets smaller over time forcing encounters. Build structures to gain tactical advantages over opponents.
