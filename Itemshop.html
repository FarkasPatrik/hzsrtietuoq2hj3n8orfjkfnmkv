Welcome to the Fortnite Information Page

Fortnite is a popular online video game developed by Epic Games. It features different game modes including Battle Royale, Save the World, and Creative.

About Fortnite Battle Royale

Fortnite Battle Royale is a free-to-play mode where 100 players fight against each other to be the last one standing. Players can build structures and use various weapons and items to outlast their opponents. The game map shrinks over time, forcing players into closer quarters and intense battles.

About Fortnite Save the World

Fortnite Save the World is a cooperative player-versus-environment game mode where players team up to fight against AI-controlled enemies known as Husks. Players explore the world, gather resources, build fortifications, and defend against waves of Husks, all while completing objectives to progress through the game.

About Fortnite Creative

Fortnite Creative is a sandbox mode that allows players to build and create their own worlds, game modes, and experiences using various in-game assets and tools. Players have the freedom to design and share their creations with the Fortnite community.

Gameplay Features

  • Building and editing structures in real-time.
  • Various weapons and items with unique abilities.
  • Regular updates introducing new content and challenges.
  • Cosmetic items for character customization.
